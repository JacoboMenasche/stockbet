Data Flow
SCRIPT 1 — scripts/sync.ts · runs daily 7 AM
  • Fetches upcoming earnings calendar from FMP
  • Stores releaseTime per company:
    bmo → PRE_MARKET (closes at 9:00 AM)
    amc → POST_MARKET (closes at 4:30 PM)
  • Fetches analyst estimates → sets market thresholds
  • Fetches stock price history → StockPriceCache
  • Creates Market rows (status: OPEN)
SCRIPT 2 — scripts/resolve.ts · runs TWICE on earnings days
Run at 9:00 AM
Close markets for companies with releaseTime = PRE_MARKET reporting today → status: CLOSED
Run at 4:30 PM
Close markets for companies with releaseTime = POST_MARKET reporting today → status: CLOSED
Both runs: after closing
Poll FMP for actual results → compare vs threshold → status: RESOLVED → write Resolution row
