Data Flow
SCRIPT 1 — scripts/sync.ts
Daily sync (runs every morning)
  • Fetches upcoming earnings calendar from FMP
  • Creates/updates EarningsEvent rows
  • Fetches analyst estimates (EPS, margins, revenue)
  • Creates Market rows with real thresholds
  • Fetches stock price history → stored for chart
SCRIPT 2 — scripts/resolve.ts
Runs on earnings day (9 AM + 4:30 PM)
  • Checks DB for companies reporting today
  • At cutoff → sets markets to CLOSED
  • Polls FMP for actual reported results
  • Compares actual vs threshold → sets RESOLVED
  • Records winning side in Resolution table
